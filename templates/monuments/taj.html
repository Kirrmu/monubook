Taj Mahal Information

Opening Hours

Open daily (except Fridays) from sunrise to sunset

Best Time to Visit

Early morning (6 AM - 8 AM) or during sunset for the best views and fewer crowds

Facilities

- Audio guides available in multiple languages
- Photography allowed (except inside the mausoleum)
- Guided tours available
- Wheelchair accessible
- Battery-operated vehicles and shoe covers provided

Getting There

Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Accessible by train, bus, and car. The nearest railway station is Agra Cantt.

Tips

- Wear comfortable shoes as you will be walking
- Carry water and a hat or sunscreen
- Plan for 2-3 hours for a full visit
- Book tickets in advance, especially during peak season
- Fridays are reserved for mosque prayers, and the monument is closed
