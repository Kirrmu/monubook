Qutb Minar Information

Opening Hours

Open daily from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

Early morning (7 AM - 9 AM) or late afternoon (3 PM - 5 PM) for the best experience and photography

Facilities

- Audio guides available in multiple languages
- Photography allowed
- Guided tours available
- Wheelchair accessible

Getting There

Located in Mehrauli, South Delhi. Accessible by metro (Qutub Minar station) and local transport.

Tips

- Wear comfortable walking shoes
- Carry water and sunscreen
- Allow 2-3 hours for the visit
- Book in advance during peak tourist season
